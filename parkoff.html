Park Off 

Phone Controlled Automated Parking App

About 


A mobile application which virtually controls all parking within one app.
The app aims to eliminate the need for autonomous car owners to worry about the availability of parking in their area or the availability of parking at their destination.


Key Features

Book Parking

More Time + Repark

Pick Up

Book Parking

Lets users select and book their desired parking spot, filter can be used to control time and price variables.

More Time + Repark

If you're running out of time you can either pay for additional time or choose to repark your vehicle elsewhere.

Pick Up

And with a press of a button you can select your location and Pick Up will take you away.
